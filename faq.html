    What is TDEE?

    TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure. It is the number of calories your body burns in a day in order to stay alive.

    How is TDEE calculated?

    TDEE estimations are calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation. This equation calculates your Basic Metabolic Rate (BMR) using your physical stats (weight, height and age).
    Then, it is multiplied by your activity level in order to obtain TDEE estimations.

    Why is it important to know my TDEE?

    In order to adjust your diet accordingly, knowing your TDEE is crucial.

    How do I adjust my diet?

    Track calories. Log food. A diet is the one you can stick to.
